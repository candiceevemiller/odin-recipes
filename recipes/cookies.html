Mexican Hot Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

Makes 12-16 cookies

  • 1 stick butter (unsalted)
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (125g) AP flour
  • 1/4 cup (25g) natural cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips + some reserved for the end
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows + some reserved for end
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne powder

Steps

  1. Cut the butter into chunks, brown the butter, transfer to dish and allow to cool completely
  2. Mix all dry ingredients (minus brown sugar) in a bowl
  3. Cream butter with brown sugar until fluffy
  4. Add egg and vanilla extract, continue to beat until mixed
  5. Add dry ingredients to wet, mix continuously
  6. Stir in chocolate chips and mini marshmallows
  7. Wrap batter in saran wrap, let cool in fridge overnight
  8. Preheat oven to 350 F
  9. Divide batter into balls, place on parchment lined baking sheets
  10. Bake 12-15 minutes
  11. Press reserved chocolate chips and marshmallows into top of cookies while they're still malleable
  12. Transfer cookies to cooling rack
  13. Enjoy with milk
