Candy's Famous Mac & Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 box elbow macaroni or cavatappi
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 jalapeño
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 lareg eggs
  • 5 cups fresh grated sharp cheddar (1 cup reserved)
  • Bread crumbs
  • 2 cans evaporated milk
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta al dente
  2. Preheat oven to 350 F
  3. Finely chop jalapeño (remove seeds if you prefer less spice), onion, and bell pepper
  4. Saute veggies in butter until soft
  5. Add eggs to cool condensed milk and whisk together
  6. Pour milk-eggs mixture into sauteed veg
  7. Add 4 c. of cheese, stir until melted
  8. Season sauce to taste with paprika, salt, & pepper
  9. Add mac to sauce
  10. Spoon mac and cheese into baking dish, cover with aluminum foil
  11. Bake for 20 minutes
  12. Remove foil, sprinkle reserved cheese and breadcrumbs on top of mac & cheese
  13. Broil on high until golden brown (about 5-10 minutes)
  14. Enjoy!
