Magical Mystical Cranberry Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 bags fresh cranberries (handful reserved)
  • All spice berries
  • Star anise
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon sticks
  • 1 large orange
  • 4/3 cup sugar

Steps

  1. Make a satchet to hold the star anise, all spice, cloves, and cinnamon sticks
  2. Zest and juice the orange, mix water with juice totalling 2 cups
  3. In a mix sugar, orange zest, cranberries, satchet, and water/juice mixture
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer covered until cranberries burst
  5. Remove lid and simmer until reduced
  6. Remove satchet, add in reserved cranberries
  7. Enjoy with roasted meat, as a jam, mix with yogurt, or even as a BBQ sauce base
